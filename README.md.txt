Amazon Sales Analysis – Power BI

Project Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing Amazon sales data using Power BI to evaluate overall sales performance, customer demand, and product trends. The dashboard provides insights into Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales, helping understand business growth over time.

Dataset:

Rows: ~89,000
Columns: 7–20

KPIs:

YTD Sales
QTD Sales
YTD Products Sold
YTD Reviews

KPI Cards for key metrics:
Monthly Sales Trend
Weekly Sales Trend
Sales by Product Category
Top Products by YTD Sales
Top Products by Reviews

Tools Used:
Power BI
DAX
Excel / CSV
